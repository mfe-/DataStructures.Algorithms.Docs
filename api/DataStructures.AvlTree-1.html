    Class AvlTree<TData>

    AVL Tree Recommended for tasks which require a lot of lookups and less rebalancing. If the majority of operations of the tree are insertions or deletions and worst-case orders are not expected it's recommended to use BinarySearchTree<TData> Check also the AVLTreeTest
